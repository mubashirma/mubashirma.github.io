    Undergraduate Thesis

    This thesis has been done in the domain of IoT under the supervision of Professor Dr. Mohammad Sanaullah Chowdhury. The title of the thesis is “An IoT Based Car Accident Prevention and Detection System with Smart Brake Control”. We have built a smart system in the domain of IoT for reducing the number of road accidents. Our proposed system provides real-time alerts to the driver for controlling the speed and reduces the speed by the system when a critical distance comes, also email alert is available to responsible individuals for any uncertain situation. We have used Ultrasonic Sensor, Servo Motor, Buzzer, and other hardware components. The processing unit is Raspberry Pi board. The idea of this proposal was accepted in a conference named “International Conference on Application and Techniques on Information Science (ICATIS), January 2019”.
